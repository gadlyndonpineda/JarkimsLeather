Our Story:
Building a Legacy of Leather Excellence

Jarkims Leather Corporation (JLC) is a leading tannery and leather manufacturer dedicated to producing premium leather goods that meet the highest standards. Although officially established in 2016, JLC's legacy began in 2003 when founder Eduardo Lazatin Jr. started in leather production, driven by a vision to create durable and quality products. Over the years, JLC has grown into a family-run business, supplying premium leather to diverse industries, including fashion, automotive, upholstery, footwear, and industrial sectors.


JLC's leather products cater to the unique needs of each industry, offering customized solutions that enhance product quality, durability, and aesthetics. With a deep understanding of these markets, JLC remains a trusted partner to brands and businesses seeking top-tier leather materials. Committed to ethical production, skilled labor, and supporting local livelihoods, JLC strives to continue contributing to the growth and resilience of the Philippine leather industry.

MISSION

JLC's mission is to deliver premium leather products and services that empower businesses to create exceptional products with a steadfast commitment to integrity, consistency, and sustainability.

VISION

JLC envisions becoming the Philippines' industry standard and a global leader in leather manufacturing, expanding our product offerings, strengthening partnerships, and driving sustainability to shape the future of the leather industry.
